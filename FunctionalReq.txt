Functional requirements for an online retail store project:

    Product browsing and search: Customers should be able to browse products by category and subcategory, and search for products by keywords. Products should be displayed with relevant information such as name, description, price, and image.

    Shopping cart: Customers should be able to add products to their shopping cart, view the contents of their cart, and update the quantity of items in the cart.

    Checkout: Customers should be able to proceed to checkout and enter their shipping and billing information. The checkout process should be secure and protect customer's sensitive information.

    Payment: The website should support multiple forms of payment including credit/debit cards and online payment gateways.

    Order confirmation and shipping notification: Customers should receive an email confirmation of their order and a shipping notification email once their order has been shipped.

    Customer account: Customers should be able to create an account, view their order history, and track the status of their current orders.

    Back-end admin panel: Store staff should be able to manage products, orders, customers, and other website content through a back-end admin panel.

    Responsive design: The website should be optimized for viewing on all devices including desktop, tablet and mobile.

    Multilingual and multicurrency support: The website should support multiple languages and currencies to reach a global audience.

    SEO-friendly and easy to navigate: The website should be designed to be SEO-friendly and easy to navigate to improve the user experience and increase the visibility of the store.

    Order Tracking: Customers should be able to track the status of their order and get the expected delivery date.

    Product Reviews and ratings: Customers should be able to leave reviews and ratings on the products they purchased.

    Customer Service: The website should have a customer service chatbot or live chat feature to assist customers with their queries.

    Promotions and discounts: The website should have a feature to apply promotions and discounts on the products or orders.

Note: This is just a general list, the actual functional requirements will depend on the requirement and features of the store.
