Project Scope:

Objective: To develop an online retail store that allows customers to browse and purchase products from a variety of categories, including clothing, electronics, home goods, and more.

Scope:

    Website design and development, including the creation of a visually appealing and easy-to-use interface for browsing and purchasing products
    Product catalog management, including the ability to add, edit, and delete products as well as upload product images
    Shopping cart and checkout functionality, allowing customers to add items to their cart and proceed to checkout to complete their purchase
    Secure payment gateway integration, supporting multiple payment methods such as credit card, debit card, and e-wallets
    Order management, including the ability to view and track orders, update order status, and generate invoices
    Customer account management, allowing customers to create an account, log in, view their order history, and update their personal information
    Inventory management, tracking the availability of products in real-time and updating the website accordingly
    Customer service, including a contact form for customers to submit inquiries and complaints, as well as a FAQ page
    Search and filtering functionality, allowing customers to easily find products they're looking for
    Mobile responsiveness, ensuring the website is easily usable on mobile devices

The scope of the project is limited to the above features, and any additional requirements will be evaluated and considered for inclusion in future phases of development.
